Our Events

Hackathons

Members will attend multiple hackathons hosted online by several companies and organizations.

USACO

Members will be able to attend the USACO competitions which take place during Decemeber, January, and February.

Brain Puzzles

Occasionally, members will work with each other to solve common brain teasers regarding concepts within programming.

Code Quest

Code Quest is a competition that members can particpate in to challenge their programming knowledge and skills.

Speaker Events

EVPC will have several speakers visiting this year to talk about programming/computer science in the workplace and in future education.

Quiz/Kahoot Games

In most meetings, members will play kahoot games to test their understanding of the material we covered in the meeting

About EVPC

EVPC is the official programming club at Evergreen Valley High School. Our mission is to inspire students to purse future careers in computer science.
